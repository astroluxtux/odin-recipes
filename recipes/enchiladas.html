Enchiladas

Beef Enchilada

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of ground beef
  • 1 bag of cheddar cheese
  • Flour or corn tortillas
  • 1 package of taco seasoning
  • 1 can of enchilada sauce OR Easy Enchilada Sauce Recipe (Below)
  • 1 onion

Easy Enchilada Sauce Recipe (Optional)

  • 3 T of oil
  • 3 T of flour
  • 2 T of chili powder
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 8 oz. of tomato sauce
  • 1-2 t of chopped garlic or 1 t of garlic powder
  • 1 t of oregano
  • 1 t of cumin

Instructions

Making the Enchilada Sauce (Optional)

  1. Heat 3 T of oil in a pan over low to medium heat
  2. When the oil is hot, whish flour and chili powder, cooking for 1-2 minutes
  3. Immediately reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
